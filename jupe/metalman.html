METAL MAN

This page is all about Metal Man: his steel-heavy stage, his blade-throwing attack pattern, and the easiest way to take him down before he takes you down.

About Metal Man

Metal Man is one of the most memorable bosses in Mega Man 2. He has a metal stage, sharp blade attacks, and he can be hard if you do not pay attention to how he moves.

Why People Like Him

He looks cool, his weapon is awesome, and his stage has a really strong metal factory vibe. A lot of people remember him because he is fun and dangerous at the same time.

Metal Man Stage Video

Stage Tips

Metal Man's stage has gears, conveyor belts, and jumps that can mess up your timing. The main thing is to not rush.

  • Watch the conveyor belts before you jump.
  • Take your time on the moving gears.
  • Try to beat enemies before they crowd you.
  • Stay calm so you do not waste health.

How To Fight Him

Metal Man jumps around and throws Metal Blades at you. He is easier when you stay calm and do not stand in one place too long.

  1. Be ready as soon as the fight starts.
  2. Keep a little space between you and him.
  3. Move when he gets ready to throw a blade.
  4. Attack when he lands or gives you an opening.

Weakness

Metal Man is weak to his own Metal Blade, which is one of the coolest things in the game. If you already have that weapon, the fight becomes way easier.

If you only have the Mega Buster, you can still win by dodging first and shooting when it is safe.

Quick Facts

  • Metal Man is one of the most popular Robot Masters.
  • His weapon is useful against a lot of other bosses too.
  • He gets harder if you panic and rush.
  • The middle of the room is a good place to control the fight.

How To Beat Metal Man

The easiest way to beat Metal Man is to stay calm, dodge his attacks, and wait for a good chance to hit him back. If you have the Metal Blade, use it. If not, the Mega Buster still works if you play smart and do not rush.
