About Bubble Man

Bubble Man is an underwater Robot Master in Mega Man 2. He uses Bubble Lead and can be tricky because the water changes how you jump and move.

Why People Remember Him

People remember Bubble Man because his stage feels very different from the others. The underwater parts slow everything down and make the whole level feel tense.

Bubble Man Stage Video

Stage Tips

Bubble Man's stage has water sections, spikes, and enemies that can surprise you if you move too fast. It is a stage where careful jumps matter a lot.

  • Be extra careful near spikes underwater.
  • Use the slower jumps to line yourself up better.
  • Try to clear enemies before they box you in.
  • Do not rush just because the stage feels slower.

How To Fight Him

Bubble Man jumps around the room and shoots Bubble Lead at you. He is not the fastest boss, but the water movement can still make the fight awkward.

  1. Watch where he lands before you move in.
  2. Do not get stuck in one corner.
  3. Jump carefully so you do not drift into his shots.
  4. Attack when he leaves a safe opening.

Weakness

Bubble Man is weak to Metal Blade. If you have that weapon already, the fight gets easier because you can hit him quickly and from a better angle.

If you only have the Mega Buster, you can still win by playing patient and not wasting jumps.

Quick Facts

  • Bubble Man has one of the most unique stages in the game.
  • Water changes the feel of the whole fight.
  • He is easier if you stay in control of your jumps.
  • Metal Blade is a great choice against him.

How To Beat Bubble Man

The easiest way to beat Bubble Man is to stay calm, watch his jumps, and not let the underwater movement throw off your timing. If you have Metal Blade, use it. If not, fight patiently and keep your movement under control.
