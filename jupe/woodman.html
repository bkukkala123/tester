WOOD MAN

This page is all about Wood Man: his forest-like stage, his heavy attacks, and the easiest way to beat him without getting cornered.

About Wood Man

Wood Man is a strong Robot Master with a forest theme. He uses the Leaf Shield and hits hard, so he can be annoying if you are not ready for his patterns.

Why People Remember Him

Wood Man stands out because he feels tough and heavy compared to faster bosses. His stage and attacks both have that strong, earthy feel.

Wood Man Stage Video

Stage Tips

Wood Man's stage has platforming and enemy spots that can waste your health if you just try to bulldoze through everything.

  • Take your time with jumps and platforms.
  • Watch for enemies coming from above.
  • Clear space before you move ahead.
  • Keep your health high for the boss fight.

How To Fight Him

Wood Man uses his Leaf Shield to protect himself and then sends leaves falling down. The fight is more about patience than speed.

  1. Wait for his shield pattern before attacking.
  2. Move to avoid the falling leaves.
  3. Do not waste shots when he is protected.
  4. Attack right after his defense opens up.

Weakness

Wood Man is weak to Atomic Fire. If you have it, the fight becomes a lot easier because it can do serious damage fast.

If you do not have Atomic Fire, be patient and make every safe shot count.

Quick Facts

  • Wood Man is one of the stronger-looking bosses in the game.
  • His Leaf Shield can stop careless attacks.
  • You need timing more than speed against him.
  • Atomic Fire is the best weapon to use.

How To Beat Wood Man

The easiest way to beat Wood Man is to stay patient, dodge the falling leaves, and attack when his guard drops. If you have Atomic Fire, use it. If not, play smart and do not throw away shots.
