AIR MAN

This page is all about Air Man: his sky stage, his tornado attacks, and the best way to beat him without getting pushed around.

About Air Man

Air Man is a sky-themed Robot Master who uses giant air blasts. He is famous because his attack can be really annoying if you get stuck in front of him.

Why People Remember Him

A lot of players remember Air Man because his tornado shots can fill up space fast. He is one of those bosses that feels impossible until you learn what to do.

Air Man Stage Video

Stage Tips

Air Man's stage has cloud platforms and jumps where it is easy to mess up if you move too carelessly.

  • Take your time on moving platforms.
  • Watch your jump timing around gaps.
  • Do not let small enemies knock you around.
  • Try to reach the boss with solid health.

How To Fight Him

Air Man throws huge wind shots that can block a lot of the screen. The trick is to stay calm and move at the right time instead of panicking.

  1. Do not stand in front of him too long.
  2. Watch the pattern of his air shots.
  3. Move or jump before they trap you.
  4. Hit him during the gaps in his attack.

Weakness

Air Man is weak to Leaf Shield. If you have it, the fight is easier because it does strong damage against him.

If you do not have Leaf Shield, you can still win, but you need good timing and patience.

Quick Facts

  • Air Man is one of the most famous Mega Man 2 bosses.
  • His wind shots can cover a lot of space fast.
  • He gets easier when you learn the pattern.
  • Leaf Shield is the weapon you want.

How To Beat Air Man

The easiest way to beat Air Man is to learn the rhythm of his air shots and attack in between them. If you have Leaf Shield, use it. If not, stay patient and keep your movement clean.
