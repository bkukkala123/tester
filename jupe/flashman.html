FLASH MAN

This page is all about Flash Man: his icy stage, his time-stopping power, and the best way to beat him without letting the fight get away from you.

About Flash Man

Flash Man is an ice-themed Robot Master who can stop time. That power makes him stand out because even a simple attack pattern feels scarier when everything suddenly freezes.

Why People Remember Him

Flash Man is memorable because his stage has an icy look and his Time Stopper power is one of the coolest abilities in the game. He has a calmer style, but he can still catch you off guard.

Flash Man Stage Video

Stage Tips

Flash Man's stage is not as wild as some others, but slippery feeling areas and enemy placement can still mess you up if you get lazy.

  • Do not rush through icy sections.
  • Watch for enemies before jumping forward.
  • Stay patient so you do not take free damage.
  • Keep your health solid for the boss room.

How To Fight Him

Flash Man can stop time and then attack while you are stuck. The fight is easier when you expect the freeze and keep your movement controlled.

  1. Watch for when he is about to use Time Stopper.
  2. Try not to be in a bad spot when time freezes.
  3. Move again right away after it ends.
  4. Attack during the safer parts of the pattern.

Weakness

Flash Man is weak to Crash Bomber. If you have it, the fight becomes easier because it can deal strong damage against him.

If you do not have Crash Bomber, you can still win by staying ready for the time freeze and not overcommitting.

Quick Facts

  • Flash Man has one of the coolest special powers in the game.
  • His stage has an icy style that stands out.
  • Time Stopper can punish bad positioning.
  • Crash Bomber is the best weapon to use.

How To Beat Flash Man

The easiest way to beat Flash Man is to stay calm, be ready for the time freeze, and attack when the pressure is lower. If you have Crash Bomber, use it. If not, keep the fight controlled and do not get careless.
